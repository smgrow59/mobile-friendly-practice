About the Baker

Hello there! My name is Sydney, and I am very passionate about pet health. Working at a pet store, I learned that food is everything when it comes to pets. Fresh ingredients and limited fillers can be a very large key into how healthy your pet is, reducing vet visits, making coats healthier, and more! In an effort to make it easier for pet parents of all kinds to give the best to their very best friend, I hand bake treats with all the best ingredients.

Meet the Taste Testers

These furry friends have helped me make sure dog and cat recipes are up to snuff! Since I don't own any type of small animal at this time, that treat testing was outsourced to friends and family.
